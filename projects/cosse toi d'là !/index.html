Cosse toi d'là !

23/Nov 2014
Projects Personnal

By the end of November 2014, I took part in the Game Dev Party #7, a game jam organized in lyon two times a year.

The theme was, in French: “Petit pois, gros poi(ds)”. This is a pun over the fact that pois (pea) and poids (weight) are pronounced the same way.

We created this game in less than 48 hours as a team of four: one game designer, one graphist and two programmers.

Peass off!

The concept of the game is very simple: you are a pea among four that are struggling to keep their place in the plate. Up to four player can battle and try to eject their opponents.

Players control their peas by giving them an impulsion and a direction with a XBOX 360 controller. The edges of the plate are elevated, so that a pea is attracted towards the center and needs some speed to get out.
